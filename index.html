About

I'm a 22 year old junior software developer from Berlin, Germany. I work at urban ENERGY, a Berlin startup which has the goal to bring eMobility into urban areas.

When I was a teenager my interest on technology has been growing more and more. I liked the idea of using technology in order to solve problems and to make the world a little bit better. Therefore, I decided to study Business Computing after high school. At the University of Applied Sciences in Berlin I had the chance to get to know not only the field of computer science but also about the business side with courses such as marketing.

But my real passion is programming. I love to solve real problems, being creative and to build something new. I document my learning process on Instagram, where I share my progress, tips and tricks with over 3000 followers.
