TriApp

The what

TriApp is a responsive web app which provides customized training routes in a consistent form based on real triathlon competitions.


Fast – You can quickly start your training and don't have to waste too much time finding all the information you need.


Customized – You can filter by the region, distance or discipline you want and all the information you need is included.


Consistent – You can easily get familiar with the route. Google Maps can be easily handled. The App is clearly structured.

The who

The app is designed for both beginners who want to set up a first training plan and experienced athletes who want to discover new routes to prepare for competitions.

The why

What differentiates TriApp from existing apps?

  • It provides routes or all triathlon disciplines swimming, biking and running.
  • Provides official DTU routes for the individual trainings.
  • TriApp enables the user to find the perfect route using filters for disciplines, specific distances and locations.
  • Combining routes from different websites into one application there's no need for wasting time finding the route that suites the user.
  • Last but not least TriApp provides an API for further use.
